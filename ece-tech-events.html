CIRCUTRIX

  Description :

Event will be conducted in three levels.
Level 1: 
   Here, the participant is shown an already assembled circuit and he or she is expected to reproduce the same on the sheet provided. The faster the better. Participants who are through level 1 will be given a 5 min demo on the usage and application of multisim software by the organizers.
Level 2: 
   Now that the participant is accustomed to multisim, he is provided with a circuit familiar to him on a sheet of paper. He is supposed to execute it on the software.The one who qualifies this level will be promoted to next level.
Level 3: 
   Participants are given a circuit on paper.They need to assemble the circuit on the bread board and should produce the desired output.
Note :  
   A time limit will be specified at the time of event
