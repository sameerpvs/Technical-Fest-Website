  Description :

Ignite the grey matter and explore the thousands of ideas within. We value what’s truly yours. Well, that’s what research is all about.

  • Maximum number of participants in a team is 2.
  • Abstract should not exceed 1 page and paper should not exceed 15 pages.
  • Soft copies of the submitted paper and PowerPoint presentation must be in .docx format.
  • Please bring 2 hardcopies of the submitted paper on the day of the event.
  • The team will get 10 minutes for presentation followed by Queries (7+3).
  • Persons from different institution can be a part of same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
