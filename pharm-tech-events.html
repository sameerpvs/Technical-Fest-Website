POSTER PRESENTATION

  Description :

A poster is a visual communication tool that serves as a summary of your work. So join us at our AAGAMA 2K16 to design and present your talent.

  Rules & Regulations

  • College ID is mandatory.
  • Maximum number of participants in a team is 3.
  • Abstract should be of not more than 200 words.
  • The poster should be related to any field of biology
  • Abstracts have to be sent before 12th march,2016
  • The mail should contain participant’s name, title of the poster and the abstract.
  • Selected candidates will be shortlisted and will be noticed.
  • Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team.
  • The poster size should be 1m * 1m
  • Spot registrations are also encouraged
