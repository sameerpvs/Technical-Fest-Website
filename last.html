Rules:

  1. College ID card is mandatory.
  2. Maximum number of participants in a team is 2.
  3. Abstract should not exceed 1 page and paper should not exceed 15 pages.
  4. Please bring your PowerPoint presentation in a pen drive.
  5. Soft copies of the submitted paper and PowerPoint presentation must be in .docx format.
  6. Please bring 2 hardcopies of the submitted paper on the day of the event.
  7. The team will get 10 minutes for presentation followed by Queries (7+3).
  8. Googling of the papers is strictly not allowed.
  9. Persons from different institution can be a part of same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
  10. The mail with submissions should contain:
    • Title-Theme of the paper
    • Name and contact, number of team members
    • Email ID
  11. Paper should be in one IEEE format.
  12. After you have submitted the soft copy, a panel of judges will go through your material and if you are shortlisted to give the presentation, you will be noticed.
  13. The mail should contain abstract, references, e-mail id and contact number.
  14. Spot registrations are also encouraged.
  15. Abstracts must be sent in on or before 12/3/2016.
